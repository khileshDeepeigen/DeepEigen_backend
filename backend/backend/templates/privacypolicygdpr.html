USER RIGHTS


You may contact us at any time if there are any queries pertaining to the data privacy rights or if you wish to exercise any of the following rights afforded to you under the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”). You have the following broad rights under the GDPR:

  1. Right to withdraw your consent: You shall have the right to withdraw your consent at any time with regard to the processing of the Information or personal data in accordance with Article 7 para. 3 GDPR
  2. Right to access your data: You have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether or not personal data concerning you is being processed, and, where that is the case, access to the personal data and the information specified under the Article 15 of the GDPR
  3. Right to rectification: You shall have the right to rectify any inaccurate data in accordance with the Article 16 of the GDPR. For instance, you can contact us if your contact details have changed and you would like us to update the details that have been stored with us.
  4. Right to erasure: In accordance with Article17 of the GDPR, you can contact us to delete or erase any personal data concerning you that has been stored with us.
  5. Right to restrict processing: You can contact us to restrict the processing of certain data in certain situations such as in the event we no longer need the personal data for the purposes of the processing, but they are required by the data subject for the establishment, exercise or defence of legal claims; in accordance with Article 18 of the GDPR.
  6. Right to data portability: In accordance with Article 20 of the GDPR, you can contact us to receive your data in a commonly used and machine-readable format if you want to transmit or upload it to another website
  7. Right to object how your data is handled: If you do not agree with the processing of your personal data at any time (for instance analytical proposes), you can contact us to discontinue the same in accordance with Article 21 of GDPR
  8. Right to lodge a complaint: You may lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in accordance with Article 77 para. 1 of the GDPR, if you feel that the processing of the data relating to you infringes the GDPR.
  9. Right to effective judicial remedy: You shall have the right to an effective judicial remedy where you consider that your rights under the GDPR have been infringed as a result of the processing of your personal data and the same is in non-compliance with the GDPR.
